Homepage variants

Hero layout configurations for the CHYBIK + KRISTOF homepage prototype.

  • Top-aligned hero
    Compact hero pinned to the top, level with the nav. The nav bar is transparent at the top and gains its glass on scroll; the wordmark reveals once the hero scrolls past, and the hero text hides while the menu is open.
  • Centered hero band
    Hero text vertically centred in a half-screen band below the nav. Glass nav with the wordmark visible throughout, and the top of the grid peeks above the fold.
  • Mosaic grid
    The top-aligned hero with a staggered 4-column project grid: large two-column tiles alternate sides among pairs of small tiles, packed dense. Falls back to the uniform grid on tablet and mobile.
  • Mosaic grid — natural heights
    A second take on the mosaic where images keep their natural, uncropped aspect ratios: the tile width sets the size and the height flows, top-aligned so any extra whitespace falls below. Large tiles alternate left/right for even distribution (the pull-quote row keeps its image on the right). Pairs the centered hero band with its own uncropped image set and the full text project list above the footer.
  • Centered hero + archive
    A hybrid of the centered hero band and the full projects overview page: the vertically-centred hero sits above the complete archive grid (with project codes) and the tabular “View More” project list, rather than the curated featured grid.
